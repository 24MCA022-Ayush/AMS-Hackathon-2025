Event Structure

Phase 1: Strategy Discussion

Teams will have 30 minutes to discuss implementation strategy, break down their selected title into modules, and distribute tasks among team members.

Duration: 30 minutes

Phase 2: Module Implementation

Teams will write and implement code for their modules. After 3 hours, teams receive a dataset for execution and must pass test cases to qualify for the next phase.

Duration: 3 hours

Phase 3: UI Development

Teams develop a basic User Interface to enhance the usability of their implemented module. Judges will assess functionality, integration, and overall effectiveness.

Duration: 2 hours

Terms and Conditions

Event Rules and Guidelines
  1. All team members must be currently enrolled students at Nirma University.
  2. Teams will be given a random domain through a script and must select a title from a predefined list within that domain.
  3. Teams must follow the structured approach outlined for each phase.
  4. Plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification. All code must be original.
  5. Teams must pass the test cases in Phase 2 to be eligible for Phase 3.
  6. Judges' decisions are final and binding.
  7. The registration fee is non-refundable.
  8. All team members must be present on the day of the event with valid university ID cards.
  9. Teams must bring their own laptops and necessary tools. Internet access will be provided.
  10. The organizers reserve the right to modify the rules if necessary.
