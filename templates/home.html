The hackathon will take place in the MCA Department's Computer Labs (Blocks A & B), providing participants with high-speed internet, power supply, and comfortable working stations.

Event Structure

CodeBlocs follows a unique three-phase structure:

  • Phase 1: Strategy Discussion (30 minutes) - Teams discuss implementation strategies and distribute tasks.
  • Phase 2: Module Implementation (3 hours) - Teams code their solutions and pass test cases.
  • Phase 3: UI Development (2 hours) - Teams develop user interfaces for their implemented modules.

  • 3rd Prize: ₹10,000 + Swag Kits
  • Special Categories: Best UI/UX, Most Innovative Solution, Best Implementation
  • All participants receive certificates, refreshments, and exclusive hackathon merchandise.
